# EV-Cars-Sales
EV Vehicle Analytics Dashboard
Global & India EV Landscape | Power BI Business Intelligence Project

An interactive Power BI dashboard developed to analyze the growth of Electric Vehicles (EVs) across India and the global market. This project combines data visualization, business intelligence, and forecasting techniques to provide actionable insights into EV adoption, charging infrastructure, and future market trends.

About the Project

The Electric Vehicle industry is transforming the transportation sector worldwide. Governments and manufacturers are investing heavily in sustainable mobility, making EV analytics increasingly important.

This project was independently designed and developed in Microsoft Power BI to provide a comprehensive analysis of:

Global EV adoption

Charging infrastructure
Market growth trends

Future EV sales forecasting

The dashboard enables users to interactively explore EV data using maps, KPIs, filters, and advanced visualizations.


Project Objectives
Analyze global EV market growth.
Compare India's EV adoption with leading countries.
Evaluate charging infrastructure development.
Identify top-performing Indian states.
Analyze EV sales across different vehicle categories.
Forecast India's EV market for upcoming years.
Generate insights to support data-driven decision making.

Key Business Insights
China dominates global EV sales with the largest market share.
India is one of the fastest-growing EV markets globally.
Uttar Pradesh records the highest EV sales among Indian states.
Maharashtra has the largest charging infrastructure.
India's EV market has experienced rapid CAGR growth over the last decade.
Forecast analysis predicts continued growth in EV adoption through 2030.
